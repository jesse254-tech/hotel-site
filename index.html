About Amani Hotel

For over 15 years, Amani Hotel & Suites has been a home away from home for travellers from across Kenya and beyond. Nestled in the heart of Nairobi, we blend warm Kenyan hospitality with modern comfort.

From our peaceful gardens to our rooftop views, every corner of Amani is designed to help you rest, relax, and feel like you belong. Whether you're here for business or leisure, our team is dedicated to making your stay unforgettable.
